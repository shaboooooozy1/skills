# Email Templates

## 1) Executive Decision Request

**Subject:** Decision needed: <topic> by <date>

Hi <name>,

We need your decision on <topic> to keep <initiative> on schedule.

- **Decision requested:** <option A vs B>
- **Impact if delayed:** <risk>
- **Recommendation:** <recommended option + why>

Please confirm by <absolute date>.

Thanks,
<sender>

## 2) Cross-Functional Follow-Up

**Subject:** Follow-up: <project> actions and owners

Hi team,

Thanks for today’s discussion. Here are the agreed next steps:

- <action 1> — <owner> — due <absolute date>
- <action 2> — <owner> — due <absolute date>
- <action 3> — <owner> — due <absolute date>

Please reply by <absolute date> if any owner/date should change.

Best,
<sender>

## 3) Customer Escalation Acknowledgement

**Subject:** Re: <issue> — next update by <date/time>

Hi <name>,

Thanks for the detailed report. We are treating this as a priority and have assigned <owner/team>.

Current status: <brief status>.
Next update: <absolute date + timezone>.

If you can share <logs/repro/attachment>, that will help us accelerate resolution.

Regards,
<sender>

**Subject:** Quick follow-up: <request>

Hi <name>,

Following up on <request>. We need this by <absolute date> to avoid <impact>.

Could you confirm by <absolute date/time>?

Thank you,
<sender>
